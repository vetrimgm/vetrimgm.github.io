Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Common Questions About Our Services

What legal services do you offer?

We specialize in criminal and civil litigation, property and real estate law, family law (including divorce, custody, and 498A cases), corporate legal services, banking and finance, cyber crime and intellectual property, and crime against women and children. We also handle court marriages and registrations.

How much do your legal services cost?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity and nature of the case. We offer transparent pricing and provide a detailed quote after an initial consultation. We also offer flexible payment plans for our clients.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we provide a free initial consultation to understand your legal needs and discuss potential strategies. This helps us determine how we can best assist you with your case.

How long does a typical case take?

The duration of a case depends on its complexity, the court backlog, and various other factors. We work efficiently to resolve matters as quickly as possible while ensuring the best outcome for our clients.

What makes JPR & JPR Advocates different from other law firms?

With over 7 years of experience in high court litigations, we combine deep legal expertise with a client-focused approach. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and results sets us apart in Hyderabad's legal landscape.

Do you handle cases outside of Hyderabad?

While our primary practice is in Hyderabad, we can assist with cases in other parts of Telangana and coordinate with local counsel when necessary. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
