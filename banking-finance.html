Professional Banking & Financial Legal Services

JPR & JPR Advocates specializes in complex Banking and Financial Law matters, providing expert legal representation for both borrowers and guarantors in Debt Recovery Tribunals (DRT) and Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunals (DRAT). We handle cases involving SARFAESI, IBC, and loan recovery disputes, ensuring clients receive fair and effective outcomes.

Whether facing unlawful possession, recovery suits, or financial contract disputes, our team ensures that every action taken by financial institutions adheres to due legal process. We are committed to protecting your rights with precision, transparency, and integrity.
